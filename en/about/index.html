We started in 2023 with a single goal

We knew talent would not fit on paper, so we built CatchJobs. We are creating the digital, global platform hospitality deserves.

A New Standard in HORECA: The CatchJobs Vision

Learn More In the Press

Our vision is to transform hospitality hiring globally by making Video CVs the standard. By moving beyond the limits of paper CVs, we aim to make hiring "faster, smarter, and more human." Our goal is a vibrant global ecosystem where skills and opportunities meet beyond geographic borders for industry professionals.

We bring this vision to life with our HORECA-focused platform. While enabling job seekers to showcase their personalities and skills on video, we help employers find the right candidate quickly. Led by founders with deep industry experience, we use technologies such as AI to carry our success in Turkey to global hubs like London and Dubai and to become the sector's leading community platform.

What Makes Us Different?

Unlike general platforms, we focus only on hospitality and let candidates show their personalities through dynamic video profiles instead of paper CVs.

Video CV

With video profiles where candidates can show their energy, communication skills, and passion, we reveal real potential that paper CVs cannot show.

Hospitality-Only Focus

Unlike general job listing sites, our entire focus is hotels, restaurants, and cafes (HORECA), so we match the right talent with the right venue.

Real-Time Location Matching

Thanks to advanced location technology, we help job seekers find nearby opportunities and employers reach nearby talent.

Experience from the Industry

Our platform is designed by founders who spent years in the industry as chefs and operators, so we understand both employer and employee needs.
